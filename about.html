About Intent FreeDomain

Intent FreeDomain provides free subdomains and simple DNS tools for creators, learners and developers. We focus on speed, minimal friction, and privacy. No hidden fees, no complicated steps — just useful tooling to get your projects online.

Our Mission

We make it easy for anyone to claim a web identity and publish small projects. Our mission is to reduce the friction of getting online — from prototypes to portfolios.

What We Offer

  • Free subdomains (example.int.yt)
  • Simple dashboard for DNS, redirects and HTTPS
  • WHOIS lookups and abuse reporting
  • Community-first support via Discord
