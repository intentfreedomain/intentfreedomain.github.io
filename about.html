About Intent FreeDomain

Intent FreeDomain provides free subdomains for developers, students, and creators. We focus on low friction, privacy, and easy integrations — ideal for demos, portfolios, and experiments.

Mission

Make it easy to publish work on the web without cost or heavy setup.

How it works

  1. Register an account on the site.
  2. Create a subdomain (user1.int.yt).
  3. Point it to an DNS service provider through ns-record or IP record.
  4. Then enjoy your free domain.
