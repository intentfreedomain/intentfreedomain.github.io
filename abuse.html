Abuse Reporting & Acceptable Use

We are committed to keeping the service safe. Abuse includes illegal content, harassment, malware, unsolicited bulk messaging, intellectual property theft, and actions that disrupt the service.

How to report

Please email abuse reports to abuse@int.yt with the following:

  • Your contact details (name, email)
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Exact URL(s), usernames or IPs involved
  • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, logs)

Enforcement

We investigate reports and may remove content, suspend accounts, terminate access, or refer to law enforcement when necessary.
