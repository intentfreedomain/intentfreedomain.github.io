Report Abuse

If you discover abuse (malware, phishing, copyright infringement), please use our reporting portal or contact us directly via email.

Report via Portal

Alternatively, email abuse@int.yt with the following information:

  • Your contact details and relationship to the report
  • Exact URL(s) or subdomains involved
  • Evidence (screenshots, logs, or technical analysis)

We investigate every report and may suspend or remove content that violates our terms. For urgent takedowns, include clear summaries in your email subject line.
