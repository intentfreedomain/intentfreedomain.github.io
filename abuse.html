Abuse Reporting & Acceptable Use

We take reports seriously. Abuse includes illegal content, malware, phishing, harassment, spam, and content violating intellectual property rights.

How to report

Email abuse@int.yt with:

  • Your contact info (name, email)
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Exact URL(s) and timestamps
  • Supporting evidence (screenshots, logs)

DMCA

If you are reporting copyright infringement, include detailed DMCA takedown information. We process claims and will respond per applicable law.

Enforcement

We investigate and may remove content, suspend accounts, or escalate to law enforcement if necessary.
