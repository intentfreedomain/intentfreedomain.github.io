Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14/11/2025

What we collect

  • Account info you provide (email, username)
  • Subdomain registration metadata (name, target IP/URL)
  • Basic usage logs for security and abuse prevention (IP, user agent, timestamps)

How we use it

  • To provide and maintain the service (DNS, redirects, HTTPS)
  • To detect and prevent abuse
  • To respond to support and legal requests

Sharing and third parties

We don't sell your personal data. We may share information with third-party providers for hosting and TLS certificate management, and as required by law.

Discord & support

For privacy requests or to raise concerns, contact: support@int.yt or open a ticket via our Discord: Discord.

Data retention & deletion

We retain user data required to operate DNS and combat abuse. You may request account deletion by emailing support — deletions are processed after verifying ownership.
