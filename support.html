Support & Tickets

We use Discord for ticketing and real-time help. To create a ticket, join our Discord and follow the #support or ticket channel instructions.

Discord

https://discord.gg/NCS96PS4MH — include the page URL and a concise description of the issue (what you expected, what happened, any error text).

Email

For formal requests or legal notices, email: support@int.yt

Helpful info to include in tickets:
  • Page URL where the error happened
  • Your account/subdomain (if applicable)
  • Screenshots or console logs
  • Time/UTC of the error
